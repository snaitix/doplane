Privacy Policy

DoPlane — Last updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

DoPlane ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal productivity application for task management, habit tracking, and planning. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

Account Data

  • Apple ID (User Identifier): Used for authentication via Sign In with Apple. We store a unique identifier provided by Apple — we never access your Apple ID password.
  • Email Address: Optionally provided during Sign In with Apple. Used for family invitations and display purposes only.

User-Generated Content

  • Tasks, projects, habits, events, and notes you create within the app.
  • Family group data (family name, member list, invitations) if you use the Family feature.
  • Chat messages sent to the AI assistant.

Technical Data

  • Device identifier (for subscription and trial management only).
  • iCloud sync data (if you enable CloudKit synchronization).

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or usage statistics.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking frameworks.
  • We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not collect location data in the background. Location is accessed only when you explicitly set a location-based reminder.

How We Store Data

Local Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (AES-GCM encryption). Sensitive data such as authentication credentials is stored in the iOS Keychain.

iCloud (Optional)

If you enable CloudKit synchronization (Premium feature), your tasks, projects, habits, and events are synced to your private iCloud container. This data is accessible only to you and is protected by Apple's iCloud security.

AI Assistant

When you use the AI assistant (Nova AI), your messages are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. We do not store your messages on any server — they are processed in real-time and the response is returned to your device. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for their data handling practices.

Camera Usage

The app accesses your camera only for scanning QR codes when joining a Family group. No photos or videos are captured or stored.

Calendar & Reminders

If you enable Apple Calendar or Reminders integration, the app reads and writes calendar events and reminders to sync with your DoPlane data. This data stays within Apple's ecosystem.

Location Data

Location data is accessed only when you explicitly set a location-based task or habit reminder. The app uses "When In Use" location permission and does not track your location in the background.

Subscriptions

DoPlane offers a Premium subscription managed through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Retention & Deletion

  • You can delete your account at any time from the app (More → Delete Account).
  • Account deletion removes all local data, Keychain entries, and cancels any pending notifications.
  • If CloudKit sync was enabled, you can manage iCloud data through your device's iCloud settings.

Children's Privacy

DoPlane is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating this page.
